Case Studies

Real-life cases handled by PARIHAR — names withheld for privacy.

Case 1 · School Counselling
Medical & Psychological Support for Student

A student referred through school counselling received timely medical and psychological support, preventing a mental health crisis. Collaboration between the school, PARIHAR, and a medical facility ensured holistic care and recovery.

Case 2 · Child Custody
Child Custody Resolution Without Court

A child custody dispute between separated parents was resolved through PARIHAR's mediation — without court intervention. Both parties reached an amicable agreement prioritising the child's welfare and education.

Case 3 · Substance Abuse
Family Stabilisation Through Rehabilitation

A man with severe substance abuse issues, whose behaviour was affecting his family, was counselled over multiple sessions. With support from PARIHAR and a rehabilitation centre, he returned to stable employment within six months.

Case 4 · Online Gambling
Recovery from Online Gambling Addiction

A young professional with significant online gambling debt and suicidal ideation was referred to PARIHAR. Intensive counselling, debt management guidance, and family sessions helped him regain stability over four months.

Case 5 · Cybercrime
Support for Social Media Cybercrime Victim

A woman targeted by a cyber-criminal through social media received psychological support and legal guidance from PARIHAR. PARIHAR coordinated with the cyber crime police to ensure the perpetrator was prosecuted.

Case 6 · Domestic Violence
Shelter, Legal Aid & Livelihood for Survivor

A survivor of severe domestic violence was provided emergency shelter, medical care, legal aid, and skill development training by PARIHAR — enabling her to eventually live independently with her children.
