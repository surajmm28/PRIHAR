About the Programme

PARIHAR operates three skill development training centres in partnership with the Bengaluru City Police, offering 6-month certified courses designed to give women sustainable, income-generating skills. The programme targets underprivileged women, domestic violence survivors, and police families.

The Mahila Shakti project, launched on 1 January 2024, empowers domestic violence survivors financially by encouraging electric auto driving. The InstaBag initiative promotes reusable cloth bags through vending machines, creating livelihood opportunities for trained beneficiaries.

All courses are certified, free of cost, and designed in partnership with industry to ensure graduates have market-ready skills and direct income-generating pathways.
