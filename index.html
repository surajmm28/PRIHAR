About Us

A Police Initiative
Built on Compassion

PARIHAR is a charitable institution under the Bengaluru City Police, registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act of 1960. Established in 1993, it was born from the understanding that justice extends far beyond the courtroom.

Guided by IPS officers and professional counsellors, PARIHAR operates across eight counselling centres at Women Police Stations in every zone of Bengaluru — making help genuinely accessible to all.

  • Free counselling, shelter, medical & legal assistance
  • Awareness programs in schools, colleges & workplaces
  • Partnerships with corporations, NGOs & welfare boards
  • Supported by Central & Karnataka State Social Welfare Boards

Our Process

How We Help

01

Reach Out

Call our helpline or walk in to any of our 8 centres — no appointment needed.

02

Confidential Assessment

A private session with our professional counsellors to understand your situation.

03

Tailored Support Plan

Counselling, legal aid, shelter, or skill training — matched to what you need most.

04

Ongoing Support

Follow-ups, referrals, and continued monitoring until you reach stability.
