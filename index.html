About PARIHAR

A Police-Led Welfare Initiative for 30+ Years

PARIHAR is a charitable institution registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act of 1960. Established in 1998, it operates eight counselling centres across all police zones in Bengaluru City.

Steered by dedicated professional counsellors and police personnel, PARIHAR provides counselling, police support, shelter, medical care, legal assistance, and skill development — all free of cost.

What We Offer

Core Services

All services are completely free and confidential for women, children, and families in need.

Counselling

Professional counsellors help beneficiaries solve family issues through expert guidance.

Legal Aid

Free legal support through Karnataka State Legal Services Authority every Wednesday.

Medical Support

Medical and psychiatric assistance provided through hospital networks free of charge.

Skill Development

Three training centres offering 6-month certified courses in tailoring and e-auto driving.
