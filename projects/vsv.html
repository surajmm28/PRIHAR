Vanitha Sahayavani

Vanitha Sahayavani (VSV) is the first community collaboration initiative by the Bengaluru City Police for the protection of women, established in 1999. It provides comprehensive support to women facing distress, available 24/7 through the toll-free helpline "Namma 100".

The VSV team comprises trained female police personnel, professional counsellors, volunteers, and a network of NGOs — offering immediate tele-counselling, emergency rescue, medical assistance, legal consultation, and vocational rehabilitation.

VSV operates with a 24/7 dedicated emergency vehicle for swift rescue operations, free medical check-ups in partnership with hospitals, and weekly free legal consultations every Wednesday through the Karnataka State Legal Services Authority.
